Revenue Estimation Model

Uses the same BSR-to-sales velocity algorithm used by Helium 10's Xray and Jungle Scout. Formula: Daily Sales = Category Baseline × BSR^(-0.55). Products fetched using BEST_SELLERS sort on Amazon India search, then re-sorted by BSR after enrichment. Baseline calibrated at ₹62.5L/day for BSR #1, reverse-engineered from OZiva FY2025 revenue (₹261 Cr total × ~45% Amazon × ~35% protein = ~₹34L/month at BSR #1,470). Estimates are ±40% — treat as directional, not exact. Confidence degrades at BSR >5,000 (marked ⚠). Treated as estimates, not guarantees — consistent with Helium 10 accuracy disclaimers.
